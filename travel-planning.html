Travel Planning

A smooth trip starts before you leave. Use this checklist as a simple guide while preparing for your next destination.

Before You Go

  • Confirm your transportation, lodging, and reservation details.
  • Pack weather-appropriate clothing and comfortable walking shoes.
  • Keep copies of important documents in a safe place.
  • Review local transportation options before arrival.
