Traveling to San Francisco, California

San Francisco is a city packed with hills, waterfront views, great food, historic neighborhoods, and unforgettable landmarks. It is an ideal destination for travelers who want a compact city with plenty to explore.

Things to Do in San Francisco

San Francisco is filled with culture, fine eating, nightlife and more. From beaches and parks, museums, bike riding, cable cars, to street performers, there is always something to do. The hard part is deciding how you will spend your time.

Tour Alcatraz Island

Take a ferry across the bay and tour the famous former federal prison. The audio tour is one of the best ways to understand the island's history and its place in San Francisco lore.

Visit Fisherman's Wharf

Walk along the waterfront, watch the sea lions, grab fresh seafood, and enjoy views of the bay. It is touristy, lively, and still worth a visit.

Ride a Bike around San Francisco

Rent a bike and explore the Embarcadero, Golden Gate Park, or the ride across the Golden Gate Bridge. Bring layers because the weather can change quickly.

San Francisco Travel Tips

San Francisco has mild temperatures all year long, but do not be fooled by the idea of sunny California. The city can be chilly, especially at night or when the wind picks up, so bring a jacket.

Many neighborhoods are walkable, but the hills are serious. Plan your route ahead of time, use public transportation when it helps, and leave extra time for popular attractions.
